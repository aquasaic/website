Our Story

Aquasaic was born from a shared passion between two visionaries, Shalmalee Pandit and Nikita Patil, who grew up in regions where water scarcity and pollution were daily realities. Together, they've dedicated their lives to creating a sustainable solution that not only cleans water but restores ecosystems.

How Our Technology Works

Bioengineered Yeast

Our proprietary yeast absorbs heavy metals and contaminants from wastewater, transforming it into clean water fit for reuse.

Energy-Efficient & Eco-Friendly

Aquasaic reduces energy consumption by 60%, while also promoting sustainable practices through non-toxic methods.

Scalable Across Industries

Meet The Team

Growing up in a water-scarce region, Shalmalee understood firsthand the importance of clean water. With a PhD from MIT in bioengineering, she combines her expertise and passion to develop technologies that bring life to polluted water bodies.
