Our Story

Aquasaic was born from a shared passion between two visionaries, Shalmalee Pandit and Nikita Patil, who grew up in regions where water scarcity and pollution were daily realities. Together, they've dedicated their lives to creating a sustainable solution that not only cleans water but restores ecosystems.

Solving a Global Crisis

Over 80% of industrial wastewater is released untreated into the environment. Aquasaic tackles this with breakthrough technology that can be deployed across industries.

How Our Technology Works

Bioengineered Yeast

Our proprietary yeast absorbs heavy metals and contaminants from wastewater, transforming it into clean water fit for reuse.

Energy-Efficient & Eco-Friendly

Aquasaic reduces energy consumption by 50%, while also promoting sustainable practices through non-toxic methods.

Scalable Across Industries

Our technology is adaptable to various industries, including mining, manufacturing, and energy production, making it an ideal solution for large-scale water management.

Meet Our Visionary Founders

Dr. Shalmalee Pandit, PhD

Co-founder & CEO

Dr. Pandit's journey in water conservation began in her childhood, growing up in a water-scarce region of India. This early exposure ignited a lifelong passion for solving global water challenges. With a PhD in Bio-Engineering from MIT, Shalmalee brings a unique blend of technical expertise and strategic vision to Aquasaic.

Her work at Accenture in strategy and systems integration honed her skills in applying cutting-edge technology to real-world problems. Shalmalee's research in multi-omics datasets for ecological insights and climate adaptation has been pivotal in developing Aquasaic's innovative approach to water treatment.

"Water is life's essential building block. At Aquasaic, we're not just cleaning water; we're preserving the future of our planet and its inhabitants."

Dr. Nikita Patil, PhD

Dr. Patil's roots in India's industrial belt exposed her to the devastating effects of untreated wastewater on communities and ecosystems.
